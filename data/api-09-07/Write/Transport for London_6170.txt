Transport for London
Information on all forms of public transport in London, routes, maps, journey planner and online tickets sales. Includes guide to what to see and do and visit. The &#039;Tube this weekend&#039; feed contains information on planned line and station closures for the coming weekend. This allows people to plan their journeys and avoid disruption. Our &#039;station location&#039; feed is a Geo-coded KML feed of most London Underground, DLR and London Overground stations. The findaride KML feed contains details of licensed London minicab and other private hire operators. It is based on the same database as the award-winning &#039;Cabwise&#039; service.
