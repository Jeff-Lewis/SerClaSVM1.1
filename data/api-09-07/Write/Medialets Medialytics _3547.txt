Medialets Medialytics 
Medialytics Application Management API allows for programmatic and bulk registration of iPhone, Blackberry and Android apps into Medialets&#039; Medialytics platform. Medialets is a creative ad network and analytics provider for native mobile applications, primarily focused on the Apple iPhone. Medialytics is a tool that enables you to gain deep insights into how your users interact with your apps. Public documentation of the API is not available on the site.
