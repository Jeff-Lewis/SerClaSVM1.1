Russian business project management service
PROMOpark
PROMOpark is a Russian service for business process facilitation. It helps manage projects, automate routine processes, and extract important information from data sets. The PROMOpark API provides SOAP-based access to PROMOpark&#039;s project management functions.

The website and all documentation is in Russian. However, the SOAP methods are given in English.
