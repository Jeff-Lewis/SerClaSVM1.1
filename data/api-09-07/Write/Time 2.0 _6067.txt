Online time tracking and reporting service
Time 2.0 
The Time 2.0 Service is an online time tracking and reporting service for business and personal use. It is designed to support the time tracking and reporting needs of accounting firms, law firms, professional consultants, trade contractors and in-house corporate service organizations. The Time 2.0 Service is available through a standard SOAP-compliant web service interface.  Web services offer business level subscribers with a programmatic means of accessing and managing their time utilization data.
