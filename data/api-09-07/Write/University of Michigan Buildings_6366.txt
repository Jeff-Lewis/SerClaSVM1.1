University of Michigan Buildings
The University of Michigan, a state university in Ann Arbor, Michigan, offers a variety of APIs that allow developers to access and integrate various data from the University of Michigan with other applications and to create new applications.

The University of Michigan APIs include UM&#039;s building information. The API returns buildings by name, ID, and neighboring buildings on the University of Michigan&#039;s campus. Interested developers should sign up here for more information: http://developer.it.umich.edu/node/8.
