U Mini Me
U Mini Me is a service to compress long URLs for easy sharing with friends. Compressing long URLs into a umini.me is beneficial when having to post links on Twitter and other messaging systems where text length is limited.

umini.me can be accessed both through its main website, and through its open and free RESTful API. Pasting a umini.me on the site allows users to preview it before visiting it. The focus of umini.me is simplicity and speed.
