Freespee
Freespee is a call tracking and pay-per-call service based in Europe. Some features of Freespee&#039;s services include combining call metrics, call tracking and analytics, and integration into CRM and CMS platforms.

The Freespee API allows developers to integrate and build on Freespee&#039;s functionality and access the information from Freespee.
