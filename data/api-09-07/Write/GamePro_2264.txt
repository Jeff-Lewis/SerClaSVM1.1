Gaming review service
GamePro
Gamepro offers a database of content for gamers and developers that includes: 

    * 11,000 game titles on 20 different game platforms
    * 18 types of meta data for most game titles such as video, screens, and ratings
    * 250 news articles and game reviews being written each month

The API lets you develop a custom feed of any element of GamePro&#039;s content database that you want. Available data includes Game Catalog Data, Ratings and Reviews, Industry News, Video and Screens, and Cheats.
