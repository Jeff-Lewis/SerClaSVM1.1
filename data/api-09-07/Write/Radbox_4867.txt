Video bookmarking and saving service
Radbox
Radbox is a service that allows users to bookmark and save videos from numerous video websites (YouTube, Facebook, CNN, etc.) to watch later. Users can also save videos via email and Google Reader, and share the videos with others.

The Radbox API allows developers to access Radbox&#039;s functionality to add videos, either individually or in bulk, to Radbox.
